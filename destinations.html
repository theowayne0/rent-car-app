Discover North Cyprus

Explore breathtaking destinations that make your journey unforgettable.

Kyrenia Castle

One of the most iconic landmarks in North Cyprus, Kyrenia Castle boasts a rich history and stunning architecture. Perfect for history enthusiasts.

Long Beach

Relax and unwind at Long Beach, known for its pristine sands and crystal-clear waters. Ideal for families and sun-seekers.

Bellapais Abbey

Located in the tranquil hills of Kyrenia, Bellapais Abbey offers serene beauty and panoramic views, making it a favorite for photographers.

St. Hilarion Castle

Often called the "Disney Castle of Cyprus," St. Hilarion offers breathtaking views and a magical experience for adventurers.

Salamis Ancient Ruins

Step back in time with a visit to the Salamis Ruins, an ancient city with impressive amphitheaters and Roman baths.
